Notice

  1. Everybody Pays
    PyCon APAC 2014 is a paid conference; everybody including staff and speakers has to pay to attend (see PyCon: Everybody Pays).
     
  2. To Pay for Tickets
    You can pay through credict cards, PayPal, and ATM transfer (might only valid in Taiwan).
     
  3. Financial Aid Plan
    If you are struggling with finances and can't afford a ticket, please contact us for Financial Aid, but you need to purchase the ticket before applying for financial aid.
    More info about applications on this page.
     
  4. No Refund
    There would be no refund once registration is confirmed. But we accept upgrade from individual ticket to corporate ticket before the end of ticket sales (prior to May 2nd). Please send your upgrade request to registration[at]pycon.tw.
     
  5. Special Food Taboos
    We will provide lunch during conference. Let us know if you are a vegetarian or non-pork eaters.
     
  6. Valid Contact Info
    Please leave valid contact information (mobile phone number and email) during registration, so you can get latest updates from us.
     
  7. Ticketing Consultancy
    Please kindly mail to registration[at]pycon.tw if you have any question about ticketing. David Chiu, who is in charge of ticketing, will reply you ASAP!
