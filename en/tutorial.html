PyCon APAC 2014 Tutorial

Tutorial track is part of the conference program. Each tutorial is one day (6-hour) class, arranged to run within the week ahead of conference days. After the call for proposals, we accept the following 3 tutorials:

  • [Advanced] PlaY Data, by c3h3, 2014/05/10 (Sat)
  • [Beginner] Introduction to Django, by Andy Dai, 2014/05/16 (Fri)
  • [Advanced] Anatomy of a Data Analytics MVP, by Ken Hu, 2014/05/16 (Fri)

How to Register Tutorials

Tutorial registration is not included in regular conference registration prices. The tutorial rate is $5000 NTD each. Please register and pay on the KKTIX pages:

  • http://pycontw.kktix.cc/events/play-data
  • http://pycontw.kktix.cc/events/django-intro
  • http://pycontw.kktix.cc/events/data-analytics

Cancellation of Class

Minimum enrollment is 5, otherwise we may consider cancel the class. Once a tutorial is cancelled, we will refund 100% of the payment.
